Campaign overview — Spring 2026

The following outlines the proposed marketing plan from April 2 through Mother's Day on May 10. The campaign is divided into two phases: a Routine Bundle push in early April, followed by a Mother's Day Collection Box campaign beginning mid-April. Please review each phase below and share any feedback or requested changes.

Phase 1 — The Routine Push April 2 – April 18
▼
Featured products

The Routine Bundle — pouches, shampoo/body wash, conditioner, and body butter.

Promotional mechanic

Free gift with purchase through April 12, creating urgency with a hard end date. Bundle savings advertised in ad copy with no additional discount beyond standard bundle pricing.
